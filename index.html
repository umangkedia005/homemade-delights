Our Story

Baked with Love & Tradition

At Homemade Delights, every cake is a labour of love. We believe in using only the finest ingredients—no preservatives, no shortcuts—just pure, honest baking straight from our home kitchen to your table.

Founded by Roshni Kedia, we serve the sweet-toothed residents of Giridih with over 30 unique cake varieties, from classic Vanilla to exotic Rasmalai and traditional Aata Jaggery.
